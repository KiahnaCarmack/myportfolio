About Me

I am a 23 year old graduate from the University of Washington Coding Bootcamp,Class of 2022. I moved from Austin, Texas to Seattle in 2019. I am a food lover, animal lover, adventurer, and take every opportunity to try something new. I aspire to push myself as far as I can go in life and will work to maximize every skill and experience I have. I transitioned from working in restaurants to working in Property Management a few months after transitioning to the Emerald City. After three years of building relationships with my residents, web development was a trending topic in many of my conversations. I was encouraged on multiple occasions to take classes and programs to get into the industry. Finally, in 2021, I had another resident speak powerful words of encouragement to push me to take a leap of faith. With the support of my family, friends, and dogs of course, I enrolled. Here I am 6 months later with newly developed skills that I am ecstatic to apply and polish with time.
